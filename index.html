About

Mehfil-e-Sama’a is a Delhi based Sufi-Hindustani classical music ensemble mostly comprised of artists from Kerala who are settled in Delhi. It has already performed in around 90 stages across India and there are active discussions going on for stages in Middle East and South Asia.
